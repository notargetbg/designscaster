The creator of this project is Zheka. Having few years of background in seo he transitioned strictly into web development in the summer of 2012. At the fall of 2014 Zheka decided to start gathering a team of his own and founded DesignsCaster. Now DesignsCaster is alive and already casts beautiful designs! The mission of DC is to improve and expand in every aspect of the web development business.
